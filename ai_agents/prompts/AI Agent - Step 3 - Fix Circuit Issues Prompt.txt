## NET CONFLICT RESOLUTION RULES

### Rule 1: First Assignment Wins
The FIRST time a pin is assigned to a net, that's its permanent net.
Example:
- Connection 1: U1.7 → VCC
- Connection 2: U1.7 → VCC_STARTUP (CONFLICT!)
- Resolution: U1.7 stays as VCC

### Rule 2: Ground Nets Take Priority
If conflict involves ground, ground wins:
- GND > PGND > DC_BUS_NEG > other_grounds

### Rule 3: Power Nets Secondary Priority
- VCC > VDD > V+ > other_power_nets

### Rule 4: Signal Integrity
Never change established signal paths, only fix the conflicts.
